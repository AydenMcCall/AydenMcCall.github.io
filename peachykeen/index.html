A little about us

We're a family run business centered in Meridian, Idaho. It all started with three crazy cousins who decided to run a farmer's market booth together and our business has continued to grow from there.

Freeze-dried candy was an instant hit and has become our main product line. So drop on by and try one of our signature freeze-dried sweets!
